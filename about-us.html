The Beginning SangPisang

It starts with someone who does various ways to produce culinary products that are liked by the Indonesian tongue. Kaesang Pangarep and Ansari Kadir have been struggling to build the Sang Pisang business since 2017. With the initial joint venture capital, the banana nugget business in Cempaka Emas became the premiere outlet. Entering its third month, Sang Pisang has five outlets in the Jakarta area and within 5 years running, Sang Pisang has 100 outlets spread across a number of cities in Indonesia.
